Raising Resilient Teens: Empowering Youth with Tools for Growth
Thursday, September 25
7:00 pm ET
Parenting teens today comes with unique challenges – but you don’t have to navigate them alone. Join us to learn how Soluna, a state-funded mental wellness service, helps young people connect 1:1 with trained coaches and licensed counselors, offering them a safe space to build confidence, set goals, and strengthen emotional skills.
In this webinar, we’ll cover:
•  How Soluna provides safe, accessible support for teens – anytime, anywhere
•  Who Soluna coaches are, and what coaching looks like
•  How to encourage teens to care for their minds as a key part of overall wellness
